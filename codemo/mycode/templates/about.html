About CodeMo

This is what CodeMo does.

CodeMo increase diversity in computer science by reaching students of all backgrouds where they are - at their skill-level, in their schools, and in ways that inspire them to keep learing. The vast majority of the studentss on CodeMo are from student groups historically underrepresented in computer science.

We believe that a quality computer science education should be available to every child, not just a lucky few.

CodeMo website is built in the Django framework of Python. Django is a high-level Python web framework that encourages repid development and clean, pragmatic design.

History

In 2024, CodeMo was launched by Mohit Chauhan. The aim of creating the CodeMo website was to be build in Django project. Later Owner again thought of creating a CodeMo website or put all projects on the CodeMo.

The purpose of creating the CodeMo website was to share all projects with everyone. which is created by owner.
